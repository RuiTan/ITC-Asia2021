Paper Submmission

Regular paper submissions should be made electronically by PDF manuscripts only, not exceeding 6 pages in IEEE 2-column format (including abstract, figures, tables, and bibliography). A submission will be considered evidence that upon acceptance at least one author will attend the conference to make the presentation. Authors of accepted papers are also responsible for preparing the final manuscripts in time to be included in the electronic proceedings, which will eventually be published in IEEE Xplore Digital Library. At least one full registration to the conference is required for each accepted paper.


ITC-Asia uses a competitive selection process for technical papers. For a submission to be selected, it should contain sufficient amount of contribution in the relevant areas of the conference, the research problem should be clearly formulated and motivated, the proposed technique should be clearly described, and experimental results or supporting data should be provided.


Submission Website: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=itcasia2021


Each paper will be reviewed by at least three experts, and then the program committee will decide on the acceptance of the paper based on the scores and comments.
